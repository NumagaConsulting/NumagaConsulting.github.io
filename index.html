About Me

Hello, I'm Yu, based in Paris. Once a while, my wife (back in days was my girlfriend) told me that her travel agency needs a website for sales. After a while searching on market, either the cost of building site was not affordable or the time required for such job was unexpected long. I decided to make a website with the knowledge I had for her business demand.

At the time, I have been using python for data analyse and web scraping for a while. But I haven't systematically learned HTML, CSS and JS. Therefore, i started with wordpress for the site, along the way of the building the site, I found to make my site fully function that I have to buy a tons of plugins which was going to cost me much more than our budget. Morevoer, the site still didn't come out the way as we required.

I have no choice but start learning while building the site. Finally, I made it with a few essential plugins to make this old CMS to an e-commerce site, which have been online since May 2019 and over total 200K euro of transactions has been done on the site. Initially, the site is deployed with docker on my home server. Along the way, I have moved it over Aliyun which is more affordable AWS liked cloud service provied by Alibaba, a global leading Chinese tech giant.

After 5 years of military service in French foreign legion, i decided to to back to my civilian life. And I right hit on the period of COVID and I have plenty of free time. I started taking a course at Codeacademy to becaome a full stack developper. I wish at the end a year, i could have acomplished the mojority of the courses. I wish to rebuild the e-commance site for the company. From there, I would like to be a freelancer to help the people to transform their business in digital.

Welcome to check out my projects. If you would like someone to build a site, YU is you are looking for.
